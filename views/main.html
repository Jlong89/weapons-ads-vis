Visual Search and Interaction with Weapon Ads Dataset

Project Description

Class Project for USC CS572 fall'15. This is an AngularJS app that helps us visualize our crawled data of weapon ads indexed in ElasticSearch. D3 and Kibana are integrated into the app for creating the visualizations. OKFN Facetview is used for facet search. The project was initially insipired by the DARPA Memex Effort, to improve domain specific search and provide insight into how weapons are being sold and bought online. The dataset includes 151,677 indexed ads. Most of the ads are by private sellers on online marketplaces. The Data on Queries section above shows different queries and visualizations for answering some challenge questions posted by members of the DARPA memex project. Data for visualizations are queried and loaded dynamically for practice to query ES and processing the responses. You can use FacetView Search to look for different ads based on different facets or through words search.
